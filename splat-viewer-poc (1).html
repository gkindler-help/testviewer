Kitchen & Dining — What I'm Looking For

In a renovated kitchen, the finish work tells the real story. I look at:


Cabinets — Are the gaps even? Do doors close flush? Cheap installs skip the shimming.


Counters — Run your hand along the seam. A visible ridge means a DIY install, not a professional one.


Backsplash — Grout lines should be consistent. Lippage (one tile higher than its neighbor) is a red flag.


Lighting — Under-cabinet lighting is cheap. If it's missing in a "fully renovated" kitchen, they cut corners somewhere else too.
